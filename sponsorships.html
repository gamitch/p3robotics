Sponsorships

Why Sponsor P3?

Our team relies on sponsors to help fund the materials, equipment, and travel expenses necessary to build and compete with our robot. Sponsorships allow us to cover costs for components like motors, sensors, and control systems, as well as the registration fees and travel for regional, state, and national competitions. Beyond financial support, sponsors can also provide valuable exposure and mentorship, helping us develop technical skills and connect with industry professionals. With a sponsor's support, we can continue to grow and sustain our team year after year, empowering students to pursue careers in STEM while strengthening our local community.
